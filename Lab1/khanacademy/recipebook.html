Creamy Garlic Pasta

  • Time: 15–20 min
  • Serves: 2
  • Level: easy

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pasta200 g
Garlic2 cloves
Butter or olive oil1 tbsp
Milk/cream (or yogurt)150 ml

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft.
  2. In a pan, warm butter/oil and lightly cook chopped garlic (don’t burn it).
  3. Add milk/cream, stir 1–2 minutes until it looks creamy.
  4. Mix in pasta, stir well, add salt/pepper to taste.
  5. Optional: sprinkle cheese or herbs if you have.

Crunchy Tuna Sandwich

  • Time: 7–10 min
  • Serves: 1–2
  • Level: zero effort

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Bread2 slices
Tuna (canned)1 small can
Greek yogurt or mayo1–2 tbsp
Cucumber / lettucea few slices

Steps

Mix tuna with yogurt/mayo. Add a pinch of salt + pepper. Put it on bread, add cucumber/lettuce, close and eat. If you want extra crunch — toast the bread first.

Lazy Yogurt Berry Bowl

  • Time: 3–5 min
  • Serves: 1
  • Level: no cooking

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Yogurt200 g
Berries (any)1 handful
Honey1 tsp
Granola / nuts2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Put yogurt into a bowl.
  2. Top with berries.
  3. Add honey and granola/nuts.
  4. Eat immediately (best texture).
